# 🌍 Empowering Afghan Women Through Remote Work

> A platform dedicated to helping Afghan women gain skills, access global remote work opportunities, and achieve financial independence.

---

## 💡 Overview

This project aims to build a **web and mobile platform** where Afghan women can:

- Learn in-demand digital skills  
- Create professional profiles and portfolios  
- Access remote job opportunities from global clients  
- Earn income securely through an integrated payment system  
- Receive support through sponsorships and donations  

Our mission is simple:

> **Empowering Afghan Women Through Remote Work**

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🎓 Learning & Skill Development
- English language training  
- Courses in design, programming, translation, and data entry  
- Structured learning paths (Beginner → Professional → Marketplace Ready)

---

### 👩‍💻 Freelancer Marketplace
- Create and manage freelancer profiles  
- Upload portfolios and showcase skills  
- Apply for jobs or offer services (Gig system)  
- Work with international clients  

---

### 💬 Communication
- Built-in messaging system  
- Secure client–freelancer interaction  

---

### 💰 Payments & Wallet System
- Secure **Escrow system**  
- Internal **wallet for all users**  
- Support for:
  - Crypto payments  
  - Hawala withdrawals  

#### Payment Flow:
1. Client deposits funds  
2. Funds are held in escrow  
3. Work is completed  
4. Client approves  
5. Funds released to freelancer wallet  

---

### ⚖️ Dispute Resolution System
- Handles conflicts between clients and freelancers  
- Evidence-based review (messages, files, milestones)  
- Neutral decision by platform moderators  
- Secure and fair fund distribution  

---

### ❤️ Sponsorship & Donations
- Sponsor Afghan women’s education and growth  
- Monthly support model (e.g., $100 sponsorship)  
- Track progress of sponsored individuals  
- General donation system for platform growth  

---

## 🎯 Mission
